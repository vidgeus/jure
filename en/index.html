Practice Areas

Specialized in all aspects of property law

Law of Obligations

Our office provides legal assistance and advice in the field of the law of obligations, including drafting and reviewing contracts, protecting the rights of contracting parties, debt collection, damage compensation, and representation in judicial and extrajudicial proceedings.

Property Law

Our office provides legal assistance in all matters of property law, including the acquisition and protection of ownership, land registry proceedings, regulation of property relations, and representation in judicial and extrajudicial proceedings.

Inheritance Law

Our office provides legal assistance in all matters of inheritance, the drafting of wills, probate proceedings, and the protection of heirs' rights in judicial and extrajudicial proceedings.

Administrative Proceedings

Our office provides legal assistance in administrative proceedings before the competent authorities of the Republic of Croatia, with a special focus on regulating the status of foreign nationals in Croatia (temporary or permanent residence permits, family reunification).
